üí° AI Prompt Engineering Tips

Be Specific About Context

Include target audience, learning objectives, time constraints, and platform details in your prompts for more relevant outputs.

Request Multiple Formats

Ask for content in different formats (text, visual descriptions, interaction scripts) to create richer learning experiences.

Include Assessment Criteria

Always specify how learner success should be measured and what good performance looks like.

Test and Iterate

Use generated content with real learners and refine your prompts based on what works and what doesn't.

Combine Multiple Prompts

Use different prompts for different aspects of your activity (content, assessment, feedback) then integrate the results.

Consider Accessibility

Include accessibility requirements in your prompts to ensure inclusive learning experiences from the start.
